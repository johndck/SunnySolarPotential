How Sunny works

1

Sunny uses Solar potential data collated by Google.

Sunny is not endorsed by or endorses Google Solar data. This is the first release of the service to test whether this is a viable offer for consumers.

2

How to use

We don't record any personal information. Simply enter a UK postcode in the search box. Sunny will look up the available data and, if available, will present solar potential information for the requested location. Be aware, the Google data might not be 100% accurate.

3

What Solar potential data will be shown?

Sunny provides 4 key pieces of information. The estimated number of solar panels that your building could support, the yearly sunshine hours for your location, the total available space for panels and an estimate of the carbon offset installing solar panels.

Sunny Info

By making Solar potential data available, Sunny hopes to offer unbiased data to help consumers think about whether to invest in solar energy for their home.
